Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Sale items can be returned within 14 days. Returns are free for customers in South Africa.

How do I determine my size?

+

Check our detailed size guide on each product page. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit. You can also contact our styling team for personalized size recommendations.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery takes 7-14 business days and shipping costs vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website's tracking page or the carrier's website to monitor your delivery.
